About Tom & Jerry

Tom and Jerry is an American cartoon series about a hapless cat's never-ending pursuit of a clever mouse.

Characters

The main characters are mainly Tom and Jerry, and sometimes their owner Mammy Two Shoes.Tom is always trying to catch Jerry, but it backfires in a comical way.
